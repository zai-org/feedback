# 行为准则 · Code of Conduct

> 基于 [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) 改编。
> Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## 我们的承诺 · Our Pledge

为了营造开放、友好的环境，我们承诺无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残障、族裔、性征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育背景、社会经济状况、国籍、相貌、种族、宗教或性取向如何，参与本社区都是一段无骚扰的体验。

*We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.*

## 我们的标准 · Our Standards

**正面行为 / Positive behaviors:**
- 对他人展现同理心和善意 / Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- 尊重不同的观点和经验 / Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- 给予并优雅地接受建设性反馈 / Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- 承担责任、为受影响者道歉、从中学习 / Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes

**不可接受的行为 / Unacceptable behaviors:**
- 使用性化语言或图像，进行任何形式的性关注或挑逗 / Sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention
- 挑衅、侮辱或贬损评论，人身或政治攻击 / Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- 公开或私下骚扰 / Public or private harassment
- 未经许可公布他人物理或电子地址等私人信息 / Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- 其他在专业场合下被合理认定为不当的行为 / Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 执行 · Enforcement

如遇违反本准则的行为，可通过邮件私下联系维护者：**cyunying090@gmail.com**。
*Report abuse privately to the maintainers at **cyunying090@gmail.com**.*

所有举报都会被审慎、保密地处理。维护者有权也有责任删除、编辑或拒绝任何不符合本准则的评论、commit、代码、issue 等内容，并可临时或永久封禁违规者。

*All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.*
